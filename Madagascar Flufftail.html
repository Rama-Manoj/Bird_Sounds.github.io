Madagascar Flufftail

The Madagascar Flufftail (Sarothrura insularis) is a species of bird in the family Sarothruridae. It is endemic to Madagascar. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests and subtropical or tropical moist montane forests.

Little is known about this bird species due to its elusive nature and the remote regions it inhabits in Madagascar. The Madagascar Flufftail faces threats from habitat loss and degradation.
