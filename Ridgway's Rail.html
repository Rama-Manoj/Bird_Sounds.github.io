Ridgway's Rail

Ridgway's Rail (Rallus obsoletus), formerly known as the California Clapper Rail, is a secretive marsh bird native to the Pacific coast of North America. It has a mottled brown plumage, long bill, and short tail.

Ridgway's Rails inhabit salt marshes and estuaries, where they forage for invertebrates and small fish. They are listed as a threatened species due to habitat loss, degradation, and predation by non-native species.
