Yellow-crowned Parakeet

The Yellow-crowned Parakeet, also known as the Yellow-fronted Parakeet, is a small parrot species endemic to New Zealand. It is characterized by its vibrant plumage and distinctive yellow crown.

These parakeets are commonly found in native forests and coastal areas, where they feed on seeds, fruits, flowers, and nectar. Despite being threatened by habitat loss and introduced predators, conservation efforts aim to protect and restore their populations.
