Bicknell's Thrush

Bicknell's Thrush (Catharus bicknelli) is a small migratory songbird that breeds in the montane forests of northeastern North America. It has a brownish plumage, white underparts, and prominent eye-rings.

These thrushes forage on the forest floor for insects, spiders, and berries. Bicknell's Thrush is considered vulnerable due to habitat loss, climate change, and disturbance to its breeding and wintering grounds.
