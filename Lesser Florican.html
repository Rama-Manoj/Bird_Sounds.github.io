Lesser Florican

The Lesser Florican (Sypheotides indicus) is a critically endangered bird species found in the Indian subcontinent. It is known for its elaborate courtship display, where males perform aerial displays and booming calls during the breeding season.

Lesser Floricans inhabit grasslands and open plains where they feed on insects and grass seeds. They are highly sensitive to habitat loss and degradation, and their population has declined significantly in recent years.
