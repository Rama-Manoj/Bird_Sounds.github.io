Nilgiri Blue Robin

The Nilgiri blue robin (Sholicola major) is a species of bird in the family Muscicapidae. It is endemic to the Western Ghats in southwestern India. Its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical moist montane forests.

The Nilgiri blue robin is a small, vibrant blue bird with a distinctive white belly and bright blue feathers. It is often found hopping on the forest floor in search of insects and small invertebrates.
