California Gnatcatcher

The California Gnatcatcher (Polioptila californica) is a small songbird found in coastal sage scrub habitats of California and Baja California. It has a distinctive long tail and a slender bill.

California Gnatcatchers feed on insects and spiders, often foraging in low vegetation. They build cup-shaped nests from plant fibers and spider webs. Habitat loss and degradation threaten the populations of this species.
