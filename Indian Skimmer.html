Indian Skimmer

The Indian Skimmer (Rynchops albicollis) is a bird native to South Asia. It is a striking bird with black upperparts and a white lower body. Its most distinctive feature is its large red bill with a lower mandible longer than the upper one, which it uses to skim the surface of water to catch fish.

Indian Skimmers are often found near rivers, lakes, and estuaries where they feed on small fish. They are known for their graceful flight and distinctive calls. These birds are social and often seen in small groups or flocks.
