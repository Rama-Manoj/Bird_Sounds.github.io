Sociable Lapwing

The Sociable Lapwing (Vanellus gregarius) is a critically endangered bird species that breeds in Central Asia and migrates to various countries in the Middle East, South Asia, and East Africa during the winter. It is known for its striking appearance, with a black and white plumage and a distinctive black bib under its chin.

Sociable Lapwings inhabit open grasslands, steppes, and wetlands where they forage for insects and seeds. They are highly social birds and often form large flocks during migration and wintering periods. Habitat loss, degradation, and hunting are major threats to their population.
