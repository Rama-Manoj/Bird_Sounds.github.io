Madagascar Pygmy Kingfisher

The Madagascar Pygmy Kingfisher (Ceyx madagascariensis) is a small bird species native to Madagascar. It is known for its vibrant plumage, which includes shades of blue, orange, and white.

These kingfishers inhabit forested areas near rivers and streams where they hunt for small fish, insects, and crustaceans. Habitat loss and degradation threaten the populations of these birds.
