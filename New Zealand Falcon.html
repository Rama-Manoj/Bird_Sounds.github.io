New Zealand Falcon

The New Zealand Falcon is a powerful raptor endemic to New Zealand. It is known for its swift flight, sharp talons, and aggressive hunting behavior, making it one of the country's most formidable predators.

These falcons inhabit a variety of habitats from forests to open country. They are threatened by habitat loss and persecution, though they have shown adaptability to modified landscapes. Conservation efforts include protection of nesting sites and public education about their ecological importance.
